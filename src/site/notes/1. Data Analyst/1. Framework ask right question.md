# Customer
- Which customers are most likely to churn, and when?
- How do we best identify target customers? 
- What factors drive customer loyalty? 
- What perceptions do customer have of our products, services, brands? 
- What is the predicted customer lifetime value for each of our customers? 
- What service elements do customers value the most?
# Finance
- What is the optimal product / service pricing to maximize revenue growth and profit margins? 
- What are the predicted profit margins for product X? 
- What is our optimal investment strategy? 
- Which elements of our service offering can we cut to save money but without affecting customer satisfaction? 
- How can we minimize financial fraught?
# Operation
- How do we optimize our supply chain?
- Which marketing campaigns are most successful? 
- How to we minimize production waste? 
- How do we optimize our delivery routes? 
- Which products in our pipeline are most likely to succeed? 
- How strong are the relationships with our key suppliers?
# Resource
- From where do we best recruit the talent we require? 
- What are the engagement levels of our employees?
- How likely are our top-performers to leave? 
- What is our employment-brand image? 
- How do we optimize energy usage across our plants?
- What is the optimal location for our distribution centre? 
- What are our organisational behaviours? 
- What is the perception of our management style?
# Competition and Risk 
- What consumer trends are threatening our business model?
- What are our biggest IT security risks?
- Which of our key suppliers is most likely to havet delivery difficulties?
- What is the biggest thread to our employment brand?
- What are our key financial risks?
